        What's changed since your last update?
        
            
        
              Optional, but the more specific you are, the better your updated files will be. Leave blank if nothing has changed and you just want to re-confirm your existing profile.

        
              Things worth mentioning:

        
              Job search targets
        
              — New or changed target roles (titles, seniority level)
        
              — New or changed target locations, or relocation/remote flexibility changes
        
              — New or changed compensation target
        
              — New industries or company types you're now targeting
        
              — Industries or company types you want to rule out
        
              — New deal-breakers or nice-to-haves

        
              Career updates
        
              — A new role, promotion, or title change
        
              — New responsibilities or scope in your current role
        
              — A new accomplishment, project, or metric worth adding (be specific — numbers and outcomes matter)
        
              — A skill, certification, or capability you've gained
        
              — A role or experience you want de-emphasized or removed from future materials

        
              Constraints and framing
        
              — Anything you want kept out of future applications (a past employer, a gap, a sensitive topic)
        
              — A new way you want to frame your background

        
              Example: "Got promoted to Senior Director in March. Now also open to fully remote roles, not just Bay Area/NYC. Led a $2M cost reduction initiative I want included. No longer interested in fintech."
